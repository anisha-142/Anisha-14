Legacy & Heritage

About Us

Janata Shikshana Samiti

Founded in 1944 by the legendary Sri. R. S. Hukkerikarji, Janata Shikshana Samiti was born from a profound commitment to propagate value-centric and life-transforming education. Today, it stands as the preeminent educational heartbeat of North Karnataka, orchestrating a network of 22 world-class institutions and nurturing the aspirations of over 21,600 students.

This extraordinary legacy of progress has flourished under the divine presidency of the Late His Holiness Shri Shri Vishweshateertha Swamiji and the visionary chairmanship of Dr. D. Veerendra Heggadeji. Since 1973, the administration has been guided by the blessings of Poojya Heggadeji, ensuring a future-ready educational ecosystem.

College Origins

Embarking on its prestigious journey in 1944, JSS SMI was founded under the brilliant leadership of the renowned litterateur Dr. B. M. Sreekanthaiah (the 'Kannadad Kanv'). Over eight decades, the institution has evolved into a powerhouse of knowledge, establishing its esteemed Arts (1968), Science (1971), and Commerce (1976) wings to empower generations of professionals.

"Perched majestically atop the Mailarlingana Gudda (Vidyagiri) at an altitude of 2,511 feet, our campus is a sanctuary where spiritual serenity meets academic rigor, offering an unrivaled panoramic view of Lord Mailarlingeshwar and the historic city of Dharwad."

About The Placement Portal

This dedicated Campus Placement Portal serves as the central digital hub connecting our brilliant students with top-tier corporate recruiters. It is meticulously designed to streamline the entire recruitment lifecycle, providing powerful tools for students, placement officers, and participating companies.

Opportunity Tracking

Students can instantly discover, apply, and track applications for visiting companies directly through their personalized dashboard.

Curated Study Resources

Exclusive access to premium placement preparation materials, including DSA notes, aptitude cheat sheets, and technical guides.

Company Integration

Visiting recruiters have their own dedicated dashboard to manage job postings, shortlist candidates, and schedule interview rounds effortlessly.

Ongoing Training

A built-in ecosystem for students to take mock assessments and view video tutorials to maximize their employability and interview success rates.
