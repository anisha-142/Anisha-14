                    DIGITAL MARKETING & SEO ESSENTIALS
================================================================================

1. WHAT IS DIGITAL MARKETING?
------------------------------
Digital marketing is the promotion of brands to connect with potential customers 
using the internet and other forms of digital communication. It spans multiple 
channels:
- Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
- Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)
- Social Media Marketing (SMM)
- Content Marketing
- Email Marketing
- Affiliate Marketing

2. SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION (SEO)
------------------------------------
SEO is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility and ranking 
organically (without paying) on search engine results pages (SERPs) like Google.

The Three Pillars of SEO:
1. On-Page SEO: Optimizing page elements:
   - Target keywords in titles, headers (H1, H2), and content.
   - High-quality, engaging content that answers search intent.
   - Optimized image alt-text and structured internal linking.
2. Off-Page SEO: Building site authority externally:
   - Earning high-quality backlinks from authoritative, relevant websites.
   - Brand mentions and positive reviews on local or social channels.
3. Technical SEO: Backend website optimization:
   - Accelerating page load speeds (Core Web Vitals).
   - Establishing secure connection protocols (HTTPS).
   - Mobile-friendliness and XML sitemaps for search engine crawlers.

3. DIGITAL MARKETING KPIs (KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS)
-------------------------------------------------------
To evaluate campaign performance, digital marketers analyze essential metrics:
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): (Clicks / Total Impressions) * 100
- Conversion Rate: (Action Takers / Total Visitors) * 100
- Cost Per Click (CPC): Total Ad Spend / Total Clicks
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Total Revenue / Total Ad Spend
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total Marketing Cost / New Customers Acquired

================================================================================
                    DIGITAL MARKETING INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q1. What is the difference between Organic and Paid Traffic?
------------------------------------------------------------
- Organic Traffic: Visitors who arrive on your site by clicking on search results 
  that appear naturally (unpaid) due to successful SEO practices. 
  It is free but takes time to build.
- Paid Traffic: Visitors who click on sponsored listings or ads (such as Google Ads 
  or Meta Ads) that you pay for (e.g., Pay-Per-Click). 
  It is instantaneous but stops the moment you stop paying.

Q2. What are "Keywords" and why are they important in SEO?
----------------------------------------------------------
Keywords are the specific words or phrases that users type into search engines 
when looking for information. They are crucial because they bridge the gap 
between what people are searching for and the content you provide. Proper 
keyword research ensures you target search terms that have high search volume 
but manageable competitive difficulty.

Q3. What is the difference between Black Hat and White Hat SEO?
---------------------------------------------------------------
- White Hat SEO: Optimization techniques that comply with search engine guidelines, 
  focusing on providing value, high-quality content, and an excellent user experience. 
  It yields sustainable, long-term rankings.
- Black Hat SEO: Manipulative practices designed to exploit search engine algorithm 
  vulnerabilities to rank higher (e.g., keyword stuffing, link purchasing, cloaking). 
  It is highly risky and can result in severe Google penalties or blacklisting.
