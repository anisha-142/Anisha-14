WELCOME TO OUR COLLEGE WEBSITE

Greets to the temple of knowledge, JSS SMI UG & PG Studies, Vidyagiri, Dharwad. Every day we serve intendum hand in hand with parents, teachers, educators and community members to enhance and elevate JSS SMI’s Education System for the students of diverse abilities, backgrounds and potentials. Our mission is to widen the horizons of students’ academic excellence and inquisitive potentials.

The motto of JSS SMI UG & PG is to cultivate and broaden the values like universal brotherhood, men of character, to uphold the moral standards and to inculcate human values which are long lasting.

JSS SMI stands for quality education by providing an academic ambience with an experienced teaching faculty, a state of the art infrastructure, a well furnished library, and spacious laboratories besides a library for competitive examination.
